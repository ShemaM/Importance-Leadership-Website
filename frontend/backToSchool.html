On December 28, 2024, the vibrant community of Kasarani, Nairobi came together for a day filled with hope, empowerment, and new beginnings. Importance Leadership organized a transformative event dedicated to supporting refugee orphan school children, along with their parents and guardians. The gathering provided a platform for mentorship, educational support, and community bonding, ensuring that these young learners have the resources and encouragement they need to thrive. Through interactive sessions, motivational talks, and essential donations, the event reinforced the importance of education and resilience, fostering a brighter future for every child in attendance.

Highlights of the Day

We distributed exercise books, pens, shoe polish, rulers, and crayons to 63 learners.

Smiles and gratitude filled the air, showcasing the power of generosity.

Parents and guardians shared stories of resilience and hope.
