Understanding the Crisis

The crisis in Eastern DRC is a complex humanitarian emergency driven by mineral exploitation, ethnic tensions, and political instability. The region's vast resources have attracted global interests, fueling conflict and displacement.

  • Global demand for minerals like cobalt, gold, and cassiterite
  • Marginalization of vulnerable communities
  • Regional and international power struggles

Key Solutions & Takeaways

Empowering local voices, fostering inclusive governance, and promoting responsible resource management are vital for lasting peace.

Regional Collaboration

Strengthen AU and EAC partnerships for peace and security.

Inclusive Governance

Promote equal citizenship and representation for all ethnic groups.

Sustainable Resource Management

Ensure minerals benefit local communities, not just foreign interests.
